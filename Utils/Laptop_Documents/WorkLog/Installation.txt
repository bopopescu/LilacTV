Install SeeboMini

	1 Extract the downloaded file.

	2 Copy all files(backup.tar.bz2/factory_update_param.aml/recovery.img/SeeboMini-update.zip)
		to a SD card (formated with FAT32).

	3 Insert the SD card into SeeboMini device(M8-S802)

	4 Using a sharp non-conductive object to press the recovery button

	5 In SeeboMini device, this is located in the AV connector

	6 Keep pressing, and connect the power, until you see a RED SCREEN

	7 Release button and waiting for an animated Android with a progress bar showing

	8 The device will then automatically reboot, and start with the our Seebo TV firmware updating

	9 Don't remove your SD card until loading Seebo TV
